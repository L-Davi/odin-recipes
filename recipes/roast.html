Roast Beef

Description:

This savory dish is a sure family favorite.

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 pounds round bottom roast
  • 3 tablespoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons pepper
  • 3 teaspons dried thyme
  • 6 finely minced garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 celery stalks cut into large chunks
  • 4 shallots cut into large chunks
  • 2 carrots cut into large chunks
  • 3 rosemary sprigs
  • 3 thyme sprigs

Steps:

  1. Season roast with dry seasonings, place on wire rack, and refridgerate for 24 hours.
  2. Remove the roast, allow to come to room temperature, and place in oven. Preheat to 230 degrees.
  3. In a smal blowl, combine garlic and oil and rub all over the roast.
  4. Place the shallots, celery, carrots, rosemary, and thyme in a deep baking dish. Place the roast on the vegetables.
  5. Roast until meat reaches internal temperature of 120 degrees, about 3 hours. Remove from oven, cover wit aluminum foil, and let rest for half an hour.
  6. Raise oven temperature to 500 degrees. Remove foil from roast and place in oven. Cook for 8-10 minutes.
  7. Wrap roast with foil and let rest for 5 minutes, then slice into 1/2 inch pieces for serving.
